Emergency Dental Care

Urgent dental support for pain, trauma, swelling, and sudden breakage.

If you have severe tooth pain, swelling, bleeding, a broken tooth, or another urgent dental problem, contact the clinic quickly. This page is designed to help you act fast, not waste time.

Act Quickly

When to seek urgent dental help

  • Severe tooth pain that does not settle
  • Facial swelling or signs of possible infection
  • A knocked-out, broken, or cracked tooth after injury
  • Bleeding that does not stop normally
  • Sudden swelling of the gums or jaw
  • Dental pain that makes it hard to eat, sleep, or function normally

If your condition feels urgent, do not wait for it to worsen. Call reception immediately so the clinic can guide you on the next step.
