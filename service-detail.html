Insurance & Payment

Flexible payment options so you can get treatment without unnecessary delay.

At PK Dental Solutions, we know that uncertainty about payment can cause patients to postpone care longer than they should. This page is here to help you understand your options clearly, ask questions early, and move forward with treatment confidently.

Convenient Payment Methods

We accept cash, mobile money, bank transfers, and debit or credit cards. This gives you flexibility when planning treatment and helps remove delays caused by payment uncertainty.

Insurance Support

We work with trusted insurance providers including GA Insurance, UAP, and Old Mutual. If you are covered, the clinic team can guide you through the process so you understand what to do before treatment begins.

Simple Consultation Process

Not sure where to start? Reach out to the clinic directly on WhatsApp or call 0741 408584 or 0788 064268. The clinic can help you understand the right next step and plan your visit with more confidence.

Do not delay your treatment because of uncertainty.

Speak to the clinic today, understand your payment or insurance options clearly, and book your appointment before a small issue becomes more stressful or more expensive to treat.
