Professional Leadership

Dr. Patrick Kyagulanyi

Lead Dental Surgeon – PK Dental Solutions

Dr. Patrick Kyagulanyi leads PK Dental Solutions with a strong commitment to responsible dental care, patient education, and clear treatment planning. Many patients come to the clinic with pain, fear, uncertainty, or delayed dental problems, and one of the clinic’s priorities is to make sure each patient understands what is wrong, what can be done, and what the next step should be.

Why Patients Feel Comfortable Here

What patients can expect at PK Dental Solutions

This is not just about treatment. It is about making patients feel understood, informed, and comfortable enough to take the next step.

For New Patients

Choosing the right dental clinic in Kampala matters

Many people delay visiting a dentist because they are not sure which clinic to trust, how much treatment will cost, or whether the procedure will be painful. At PK Dental Solutions, these concerns are taken seriously, and the clinic focuses on explaining the condition, discussing treatment options, and helping patients make the right decision for their oral health.

Whether someone is dealing with tooth pain, needs a routine checkup, wants teeth cleaning, braces, or advice about a dental problem, the first step is usually a consultation and examination so the clinic can guide them properly.

Many patients from Makerere, Wandegeya, Ntinda, Kololo, and other parts of Kampala visit the clinic for assessment first, then decide on the next step after understanding the treatment plan.
